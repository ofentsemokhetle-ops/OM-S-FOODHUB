# OM's FOODHub

Local food service bringing affordable, home-style meals and catering to Limpopo, Gauteng, and beyond. From traditional South African dishes to modern eats, beverages, and desserts – we've got you covered!

## Quick Start
Just open `index.html` in your browser to see the site.

## About
Started in Polokwane as a small kitchen operation. Now serving communities and events with quality food, fast service, and reliable catering. Expanding across South Africa and the continent.

**Mission:** Deliver delicious, affordable, hygienic meals that bring people together, support local suppliers, and create jobs.

**Vision:** Become South Africa's most loved community food brand.

## Services
- Traditional SA food
- Modern dishes
- Beverages (kiddies drinks, fizzies, alcohol)
- Desserts
- Catering & bulk orders
- Fun fact: 15% off for first 10 customers daily!
